About My Approach

As a Human Potentialist and Organizational Whisperer, my joy comes from exploring and sharing tools, techniques, and insights that ease pain and suffering, both personally and professionally. My mission is to inspire leaders to transform themselves and the world through self-awareness, equanimity, and compassion.

My passion lies in the discovery and development of innovative tools through cross-pollination, collaboration, and experimentation. This unique blend allows me to identify and uncover individual and collective intelligence, enabling us to solve increasingly complex problems.

I believe in a holistic approach, integrating ancient wisdom traditions with cutting-edge technology to guide individuals towards self-actualization, healing trauma, and fostering genuine human connection. My work is about harmonizing logic with intuition and compassion to foster individual and collective flourishing.

Personality Tests

  • * Enneagraminstitute.com (Social-emotional-psychological personality test/platform)

    "At the heart of the fundamental attribution error is the tendency of human beings to attribute the negative or frustrating behaviors of their colleagues to their intentions and personalities, while attributing their own negative or frustrating behaviors to environmental factors. This kind of misattribution, where we give ourselves the benefit of the doubt but assume the worst about others, breaks down trust on a team."

    The Enneagram is a powerful tool for self-discovery and transformation, focusing on the motivation behind our thoughts, feelings, and actions. It helps identify repeating patterns and enables conscious choices for personal and professional growth.

    Recommendation: Start with the "Quick Enneagram test" (classical test - first one) on the website. For a more in-depth analysis, consider the RHETI test ($12).
